Black Pearl

Today the pandemic situation made all people around the world to stick to their homes and not able to come out of their homes missing lots and lots of memories,works and even joy. To overcome all those situations the team International service of rotaract club of hindustan university has come up with an initiative that brings smiles in all the rotaractors who knows the real joy of the projects. “BLACK PEARL” is an inter culture youth exchange program where clubs from different parts of the world meet at a platform and share all the cultures traditions and many things to learn,the main goal of the project will be to develop a strong communication and team bond that shows “we all the rotaractors are of one family” and to do more and more projects jointly that helps out the society in some of the other ways and serve the society with peace and happieness. From starting till date there are nearly 15-18 clubs(one from each distrct) from distinct parts of the world and creating many miracles with great projects and a great friendship. now

HITS MUN

HITS MUN is a model united nation conducted by rotaract club of hindustan university on main idea that an academic simulation of the United Nations where students play the role of delegates from different countries and attempt to solve real world issues with the policies and perspectives of their assigned country
