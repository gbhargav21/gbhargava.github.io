About
RACHU

ROTARACT CLUB OF HINDUSTAN UNIVERSITY is chartered on 18.02.2016 by Rtn.PP.Shriram durvi In the presence of “Rtn C.R Raju” District Governor and ,“Rtn.PP.Ashok Verghese” director of Hindustan group of institution. Over the years, it has been successful to attract enthusiastic students who became rotaractors by contributing to the growth of the club and implementing projects vital for the society. We also, work club in both skill development and career development of our club rotaractors in all aspects.
